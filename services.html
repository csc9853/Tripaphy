Frequently Asked Questions

What documents are required for GST registration?

For GST registration, you need PAN card, Aadhaar card, business registration proof, bank statement, and address proof of business premises.

How long does company registration take?

Company registration typically takes 7-10 working days. The process includes name approval, document preparation, and ROC filing.

When should I file my income tax return?

Individuals must file ITR by July 31st. However, if your accounts are audited, the deadline extends to October 31st.
